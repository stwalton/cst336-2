Electrical Grids are Vulnerable to Cyber Attacks

Main Page Secret Chips in Phone Locky Ransomware





Electrical distribution systems in the US are slowly being modernized switching to smart grids. The electrical metering infrastructure can cover a large geographical area, which could easily affect tens of thousands of people. It has already happened once, when the Russian hacker group Sandworm attacked an Ukrainian power grid. They are continously evolving which can allow for better security.
