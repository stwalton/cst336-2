Locky Ransomware

Main Page Secret Chips in Phone Smart Electrical Grids





Lockey is ransomware that is designed to block data access and then demand money. Like WannaCry, it has caused companies to comply and send over bitcoin for the release of the victims data. It is currently being distributed through email extensions, and the main file is hidden under ".diablo6" extention. Currently there is no stopping to the ransomware. Backing up your files is the best course of action to prevent from having to pay.
